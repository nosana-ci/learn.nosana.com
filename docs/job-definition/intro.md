# Job Definition

The <Glossary term="Job Definition">job definition</Glossary> is the heart of your deployments. It tells the Nosana nodes what to run, how to run it, and what it needs.

A deployment can consist of multiple jobs, depending on your deployment configuration. The Job Definition specifies what those jobs are: the container images, commands, environment variables, and resource requirements needed to execute your tasks on the network.
